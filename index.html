Testimonials

The Louisiana Primary Care Association would like to express how instrumental Honnete was in guiding our member health centers through the process of patient-centered medical home (PCMH) recognition. The Honnête team was always available to us via email and by phone to answer our questions in a timely manner. The PCMH submission process proved to be both a challenging and satisfying process for our member health centers, however we believe the Honnête consultants played a critical role in their recognition. They provided many resources to prepare our Primary Care Association staff to sit for the PCMH Certified Content Expert exam. Honnete's suggestions and recommendations were crucial to our staff successfully passing the exam. As a result of this successful collaboration, we invited the Honnete team to conduct in-person training on the PCMH transformation. The training provided gave our health centers a renewed mindset and confidence in the process improvement journey; thus, resulting in improved patient-centered care and health center operations.

Mike Smith

Director - Medical Practice Consultant at Amerigroup
